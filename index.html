Instructions

MemorEyes is a card memorization game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped over (face up) with each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching pairs of cards (i.e. find a queen-king pair of the same suit: diamond with diamond or heart with heart).

Click on any two above cards to play!
